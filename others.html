Beyond Academia

Sports Enthusiast

I enjoy playing and following a variety of sports, including cricket and badminton, as well as indoor games such as chess and card games. The discipline, teamwork, and strategic thinking involved in these activities are lessons I find valuable in many areas of life, including academia.

Travel & Culture

I enjoy exploring different regions and cultures, having traveled to Bhutan, multiple parts of India, and various locations across Bangladesh. Experiencing diverse traditions, histories, and communities has broadened my perspective and informs both my personal and professional life.
